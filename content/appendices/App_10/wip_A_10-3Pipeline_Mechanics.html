A.1 What Is a Pipeline?#

  • Definition: deterministic sequence of transformers + estimator

  • Why pipelines exist (data leakage prevention, reproducibility)

  • scikit-learn API consistency

  • Minimal example

Pipeline([
    ("step1", Transformer()),
    ("step2", Estimator())
])
